Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 28, 2026 · Last Updated: March 28, 2026

RentingRadar ("we," "us," or "our") operates RentingRadar, accessible at app.rentingradar.com and rentingradar.com. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from your Google account. We do not receive or store your Google password.

Property Data: Information you enter about rental properties, comparable listings, financial calculations, and analysis notes is stored in your account to provide the service.

Payment Information: When you subscribe to a paid plan, your payment is processed by our third-party payment processor, Stripe, Inc. We do not collect, store, or have access to your full credit card number, debit card number, or bank account details. Stripe collects and processes your payment information in accordance with their privacy policy. We receive only limited transaction information from Stripe, such as the last four digits of your card, card brand, billing country, transaction status, and subscription status, solely for the purpose of displaying your account information and managing your subscription.

Usage Data: We may collect basic usage information such as pages visited, features used, browser type, and device information to improve the service and diagnose technical issues.

Cookies and Cross-Domain Data: We use a cross-domain cookie on the .rentingradar.com domain to maintain a consistent experience between rentingradar.com and app.rentingradar.com. This cookie contains only your display name and profile photo URL. No financial, authentication, or sensitive information is stored in cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: provide, maintain, and improve RentingRadar; authenticate your account and manage your session; store and display your property data; process subscription payments through Stripe; send transactional communications related to your account or subscription (such as payment receipts or plan changes); and improve the service based on aggregated usage patterns. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore). Each user's data is isolated and accessible only to that user's authenticated account. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and Firebase security rules that restrict data access to authorized users.

Payment data is handled entirely by Stripe, which is PCI DSS Level 1 certified — the highest level of security certification in the payments industry. Your payment credentials never pass through or are stored on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

RentingRadar uses the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy practices:

Google Firebase: For authentication, data storage, and hosting. Google Privacy Policy

Stripe, Inc.: For payment processing and subscription management. Stripe collects and processes payment information on our behalf. When you make a purchase, your payment details are transmitted directly to Stripe's servers and are never stored on ours. Stripe Privacy Policy

Google Places API: For address autocomplete functionality. Address queries are sent to Google's Places API. No personal information beyond the search query is shared.

Airbnb (via Chrome Extension): For Pro plan users, our Chrome extension may access publicly available listing data from Airbnb to populate comparable property analysis. We do not access your Airbnb account credentials.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your property data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete individual properties at any time within the app. If you cancel a paid subscription, your account data is retained but access to paid features is restricted to the free tier.

To request complete deletion of your account and all associated data, contact us at help@rentingradar.com. Upon receiving a verified deletion request, we will delete your data from our systems within 30 days. Please note that Stripe may retain transaction records as required by applicable law and their own data retention policies.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

RentingRadar uses browser local storage to maintain your session and preferences (such as your signed-in state, selected theme, and tutorial progress). We use a cross-domain cookie (.rentingradar.com) containing your display name and profile photo to provide a seamless experience between our marketing site and the application. We do not use third-party tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics trackers.

7. Children's Privacy

RentingRadar is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. Specifically, you may: view and edit your property data directly within the app; manage your payment information and billing history through the Stripe Customer Portal; revoke RentingRadar's access to your Google account by visiting your Google Account permissions; and request a full data export or account deletion by contacting us.

If you are a resident of California, the European Economic Area, or another jurisdiction with applicable data protection laws, you may have additional rights, including the right to data portability, the right to restrict processing, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our service infrastructure is hosted. By using RentingRadar, you consent to the transfer of your data to the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes affecting how we handle payment data or share information with third parties, we will provide notice within the application.
